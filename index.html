A steady place to begin

Beginning counseling can feel vulnerable, especially when life already feels full. I offer a calm and respectful space for clients who want support that is both clinically grounded and deeply human.

I work with a wide range of concerns including trauma, grief and loss, anxiety, depression, stress management, disordered eating, self-esteem, life transitions, relational difficulties, and perinatal or postpartum mood disorders.

Areas of expertise

  • Trauma, grief and loss, anxiety, and depression
  • Stress management, self-compassion, and self-worth
  • Gender, sexuality, relational concerns, and life transitions

Types of therapy and support

I have experience with multiple therapy modalities and training supports, allowing the work to be tailored to each client rather than forcing clients into a single approach.

  • Humanistic, relational, attachment-focused, and trauma-informed therapy
  • EMDR, Brainspotting, polyvagal, and somatic interventions
  • ACT, expressive arts therapy, and HAES or intuitive eating-informed support

Getting started
